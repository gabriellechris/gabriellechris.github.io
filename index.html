As a final project for one of my courses, I used several machine learning algorithms to help predict the recovery rate of COVID-19 in Indonesia. The algorithms I used ranging from distance-based to tree-based. Fortunately, finished with an accuracy rate of over than 80%.

Morality Effect on Debt Repayment

A final project of Data Science Academy held by COMPFEST 12. Used the data of a evidence-based research of morality treatment effect on debt repayment. Worked in a team of three and got an accuracy of ~90% with a simple distance-based machine learning algorithm of K-Nearest Neighbors.
